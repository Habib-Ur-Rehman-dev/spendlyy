Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly (the "Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our personal expense-tracking tool. We are committed to keeping your financial information private and handling it responsibly. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account, such as your name and email address, along with the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect limited technical information, such as basic log data, that is necessary to operate and secure the Service. We do not collect more than what is needed to provide expense tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and maintain the Service: to authenticate your account, to store and display your expenses back to you, and to generate the summaries and breakdowns that help you understand your spending. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your financial records for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored so that it can be shown only to you when you sign in. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights

You retain ownership of the data you enter into Spendly. You can access and update your information at any time, and you may request deletion of your account and its associated data. Once your account is deleted, your personal expense records are removed from the Service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
