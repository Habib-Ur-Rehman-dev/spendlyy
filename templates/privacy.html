Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

01Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the account details you provide — such as your name and email address — and the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect limited technical information, such as basic log data, that is needed to operate and secure the Service. We only collect what is necessary to provide personal expense tracking.

02How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and maintain the Service: to authenticate your account, to store and display your expenses back to you, and to generate the summaries and category breakdowns that help you understand your spending. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your financial records for advertising or profiling.

03Data Storage

Your information is stored so that it can be shown only to you when you sign in. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential, and you may request deletion of your account and its data at any time.

04Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on a limited number of trusted third-party providers — such as hosting or infrastructure services — to run the application. These providers may process data only as needed to support the Service and are expected to handle it responsibly. We do not share your personal expense data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

05Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will do our best to respond to your inquiry in a timely manner.
